Game needs
1 playground
2 Two bars
3 One ball
4 2 scores

----------------------------

1 Playground
- Constant : size, color, limit border up, limit border down
- Constant : line separator, color & position

2 Two bars
- Constant : bar size, & color
- Init : position 1rst bar & position 2nd bar, no move
- move up > when hold key arrow up
- move stop up > when bar touch top border playground
- move down > when hold key arrow down
- move stop down > when bar touch bottom border playground 

3 Balle
- Init : size, color, speed, direction, angle, position
- Play : move ball (speed & direction)
- Bound : mirror > when ball position touch border playground position up & down (same speed, same direction & change angle)
- Hit bar : reverse > when ball touch bar (reverse direction, same speed & change angle)

4 Scores
- size & position
- 1rst player : +1 when ball x position is > 2nd player bar x position
- 2nd player : +1 when ball x position is > 1rst player bat x position
- Reset button : clear scores & stop ball motion
